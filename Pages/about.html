SHURUQ Management Consultancy was launched in 2017 with a far sighted vision and focuses on Quality Food Safety to bridge the knowledge gap in the industry about Food Safety Management System. SHURUQ Management Consultancy complies with regulatory authorities to offer Food Safety, Health Safety, Hygiene and Quality Consultancy, Audits and Trainings Services to different types of food businesses including Hotels, Restaurants, Catering, Cafeterias, Traditional Kitchens, FMCG, Hospitals, Schools, Super & Hyper Markets, Food Manufacturing, Processing, Trading & Distribution, etc.


SMC, has a complete control over the implementation and management of different kinds of food safety management systems, quality, health and hygiene systems, e.g. HACCP, ISO 22000, ISO 9001, etc. which are compliant with food safety & hygiene standards (National & International standards). Our team with 15 yrs of experience is well versed in modifying and having systems in place, resulting in the policies and procedures flexible to suit our client’s conveniences with regards to the time, days (weekdays /weekends) and their locations.


USP of SHURUQ Management Consultancy We provide an in-house hygiene personal and perform monthly food safety and hygiene services for all the food businesses to comply with the standards and follow municipality rules and regulations. Yearly service contracts are customized, where companies can save their internal cost of having a full time along with other expenses (visa, medical, vacation, sick leave accommodation, etc.) We fulfill all the qualitative and quantitative work to soothe the policies and procedures to incorporate into operations
